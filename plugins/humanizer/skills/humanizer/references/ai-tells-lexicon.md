## Pattern 1 - Specificity over vagueness

**Significance inflation:** stands/serves as, is a testament, vital, significant, crucial, pivotal, key role, key moment, reflects broader, evolving landscape, focal point

**Promotional puffery:** nestled, in the heart of, breathtaking, groundbreaking, vibrant

**Vague attributions:** Industry reports, Experts argue, Some critics argue, Experts believe, Industry observers

## Pattern 4 - Participial phrase overuse

Sentence-ending participial tails: highlighting..., ensuring..., reflecting..., contributing to..., showcasing..., fostering..., symbolizing..., making it..., allowing..., enabling...

## Pattern 6 - Formulaic transitions

That said, With that in mind, Moving on, On a related note, Building on this, Having established that, Turning to, Let's explore, Let's dive in, Let's break this down, Here's the thing, The bottom line, At the end of the day

**Rule:** Cut. Start the next thought directly.

---

## Pattern 7 - Conclusion bloat

Overall, In conclusion, In summary, To sum up, In closing

Also: any final paragraph that restates everything already said.

---

## Pattern 8 - Didactic disclaimers

It's important to note, It's worth mentioning, It's crucial to remember, It should be noted that, Keep in mind that, One thing to consider

**Rule:** Cut. State the thing directly.

---

## Pattern 9 - Negative parallelisms

Not only...but also, It's not just about..., it's about..., It isn't merely..., it's..., Less about X and more about Y

---

## Pattern 11 - False ranges

from X to Y (where X and Y aren't on a meaningful scale), ranging from...to, spanning...to

---

## Pattern 16 - Chatbot artifacts, sycophancy, and knowledge-cutoff disclaimers

**Chatbot scaffolding:** I hope this helps, Great question!, You're absolutely right!, Would you like me to..., Let me know if..., Happy to help!, Feel free to ask, Don't hesitate to reach out, I'd be happy to elaborate, Sure! Here's..., Absolutely!, Of course!

**Knowledge-cutoff disclaimers:** as of my last update, as of my knowledge cutoff, as of [date], as of my last training, While specific details are limited..., Based on available information..., I don't have access to real-time data, at the time of writing (when used as a hedge rather than a genuine temporal marker)

**Sycophantic openers:** That's a great point!, What a thoughtful question!, You raise an excellent point!, That's an insightful observation!

**Rule:** Remove all. Direct evidence of chatbot origin.

## Pattern 17 - Filler phrases

| Filler | Replacement |
|--------|-------------|
| In order to | To |
| Due to the fact that | Because |
| At this point in time | Now |
| It is worth noting that | (just state it) |
| It goes without saying | (then don't) |
| When it comes to | (name the topic) |
| The system has the ability to | The system can |
| In the realm of | In |
| A wide range of | many, various |
| With that being said | (cut, or use "but") |
| It's also worth considering | (just state it) |
| plays a crucial role in | helps, matters for |
| This means that | (cut - the meaning is usually obvious) |
| In today's [X] landscape | (cut entirely - always filler) |
| At the end of the day | (cut) |
